Shipper Hero is an easy to use online platform that allows shippers to host bids for their shipments, receive tracking updates, and rate carrier/broker performance in one convenient location.

Get Started

LET CARRIERS AND 3PL'S COMPETE FOR YOUR BUSINESS

Using Shipper Hero is easy. After you create your free account, simply enter your shipment details, post it as an RFP, and invite as many carriers or brokers to bid on it as you'd like. As new proposals come in, you can sort them by price or past performance ratings in order to select the perfect carrier for your shipment. Then, sit back and watch as they provide tracking updates at the specific times or milestones that you've requested.

  • Use the same brokers and carriers you already use today.
  • Invite new brokers and carriers to bid on your freight for better rates.
  • Easy-to-use app and great one-on-one customer support

HOT IT WORKS

1

Post

Enter your shipment details and post as an RFP. You can invite the carriers and brokers you already use to bid on it.

2

Select

Choose the perfect carrier for your shipment based on price and/or past performance rating.

3

Track

Stay updated on your shipment’s progress by receiving location updates and completed milestones such as picked up, appointment set, and delivered.

4

Rate

When the shipment has been completed, you’ll rate the carrier’s performance to make the decision whether or not to use them even easier next time.
